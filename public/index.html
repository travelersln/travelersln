What is travelers?

Travelers is a collection, which comprises 150 pieces that narrate the captivating story of two planets that survived a catastrophic event between two galaxies. The story of the Travelers collection is one of resilience, determination, and survival in the face of adversity. It is a testament to the human spirit and the ability to overcome even the most daunting of challenges.
